Welcome to Chennai

Chennai is a vibrant coastal city in South India, known for its beaches, culture, temples, and delicious South Indian food.

Top Attractions

Marina Beach

One of the longest urban beaches in the world, perfect for sunrise walks, street food, and evening hangouts.

Kapaleeshwarar Temple

A historic temple in Mylapore with beautiful Dravidian architecture and a peaceful atmosphere.

Government Museum

One of the oldest museums in India, showcasing art, archaeology, and natural history exhibits.

Must-Try Local Food

Idli & Dosa

Start your day with soft idlis and crispy dosas served with chutney and sambar.

Filter Coffee

Traditional South Indian filter coffee is a must-have for coffee lovers.

Street Snacks

Try sundal, bhajji, and other snacks along the Marina Beach and local markets.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Chennai is from November to February, when the weather is relatively cooler and pleasant.

Quick Facts

  • State: Tamil Nadu
  • Language: Tamil, English
  • Famous for: Beaches, temples, culture, food

Travel Tips

  • Carry light cotton clothes.
  • Stay hydrated, especially in summer.
  • Use public transport or cabs for easy travel.
